Our Story

Dongguan Siyin Sports Goods Co., Ltd.

Founded in Dongguan, the heart of China's sports goods manufacturing industry, Siyin has been producing premium neoprene and laminated fabrics for over a decade.

We operate an 8,000m² facility with dedicated lamination lines, precision cutting rooms and a full QC laboratory. Our team of 100+ specialists handles everything from custom material development to bulk production for clients across 30+ countries.

We are the same factory behind Dongguan Chenyi Handbag Materials — same production lines, same quality, now serving global B2B buyers directly with English-language support.
